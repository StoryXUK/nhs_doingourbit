The Doing Our Bit App

The Doing Our Bit App is a free to participate 24-7, one-stop shop for fitness, health & wellness by the NHS, for NHS and Social Care workers.

Doing Our Bit demonstrates the NHS’s commitment to prevention by championing health and wellness for the NHS’s most precious commodity; its employees.

Through the use of best-in-class technology combined with empathetic and thoughtfully curated content, the already award-winning Doing Our Bit App democratises and simplifies access to both digital and in-person preventative health and wellness activities via:

  • On-demand video workouts
  • In-person exercise and wellness classes
  • Group activities, training buddies & community events
