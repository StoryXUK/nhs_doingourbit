Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)

PTSD is an anxiety disorder which is developed as a result of being involved in, or witnessing, a traumatic event. The condition is most associated with war veterans, but it has been known to occur in members of the emergency services, or in fact anyone who has been involved in a traumatic experience.

Visit PTSD UK to find out more about PTSD, including what it is, who is affected and help available.
