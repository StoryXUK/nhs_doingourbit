Top 10 Tips

These activities are known to help boost your mood, relieve tension, ease stress and reduce anxiety.

  1. Make connections and talk

    High stress, busy jobs and remote working can leave you missing out on the social benefits of work each day and can make you feel lonely. Schedule in time for social breaks, such as chatting with your colleagues, to allow you to share your experiences and feel part of a group. Research has found the feeling of relatedness during a social break brings a positive association with feeling positive afterwards.
