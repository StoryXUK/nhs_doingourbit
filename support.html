How To Support Our Movement

We are building the largest free platform supporting the health and wellbeing of front line workers in the world and we need your help. Teachers inspire, heal, pass down wisdom, and bring us together. If you would like to be part of a community of wellbeing professionals gifting their knowledge to front line workers please register below.

#DoingOurBit Energisers are the foundation of our platform, wellbeing professionals across the country who are getting involved with #DoingOurBit.

Get Involved

Gyms & Studios across the country are opening up free spaces for NHS workers for a workout or a taster slot in a group class like spin or Zumba: can you help in this way?

Register

Leisure centres are key to the health of any community. By partnering with #DoingOurBit you can open up swimming sessions or gym sessions to employees of your local NHS trust. Many NHS employees work shifts so they can take advantage of slots when your venue is less busy. Everyone wins!

Our Partners

#DoingOurBit would not exist without our amazing partners. The generosity of individuals and organisations giving their time and skills has helped us create this unique free platform to support the wellbeing of NHS and social care workers. Added to this, we have a fantastic group of first-rate instructors and experts in fitness, wellness and mental health who have donated their programmes, sessions and advice. We thank them all!
