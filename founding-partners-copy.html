The partners who brought #DoingOurBit to life

Fantastic founders

Three generous and talented leisure sector partners – Active IQ, fibodo and Study Active - took Julie’ brilliant idea for a free wellbeing platform for NHS and Social Care workers and brought #DoingOurBit to life. Working tirelessly round-the-clock, together they brought #DoingOurBit to life in just six weeks.

Active IQ, fibodo and Study Active all gave their time and skills freely. Meanwhile, Action Group brought the partners together, managed the project and co-ordinated the first wave of PTs to establish #DoingOurBit. The PTs worked to exacting criteria set by Study Active and every minute of every workout was viewed by the Active IQ external verifying team to ensure they were safe and effective. fibodo skilfully designed the platform in double-quick time.

Julie and the founding partners worked tirelessly round-the-clock to bring #DoingOurBit to life in just six weeks. Their unwavering support since day one continues as they steer #DoingOurBit to ensure it remains valid and valuable long into the future.

Julie Davis - #DoingOurBit founder

Julie is a champion amateur powerlifter and soon-to-be personal trainer who strongly advocates exercise for physical and mental wellbeing.
“When the coronavirus pandemic struck it was a worrying time for everyone, especially health workers out on the frontline. New respect for the NHS was welcome but thanking the NHS wasn’t enough on its own. Working flat out to help keep us all safe, NHS workers needed support to keep fit and well themselves. #DoingOurBit started as a passion project for me and I couldn’t have imagined how quickly it would grow. The fact we now have over 100 NHS trusts signed up and over 700,000 NHS staff eligible to access #DoingOurBit just goes to show how much it’s needed and valued.”

fibodo

fibodo is a leading e-commerce platform for services that empower the world’s most active communities by connecting fitness professionals with clients and leisure facilities.

“Our team worked round the clock to create the bespoke pages and ensure ease of accessibility for users on an easy-to-use platform,” says Anthony Franklin, CEO, fibodo. “The passion and enthusiasm of the whole project team was a massive incentive to get involved and we are delighted to be doing our bit, giving NHS staff the opportunity to access the best in fitness training. Behind the warm welcome on the #DoingOurBit platform sits a robust system that is constantly upscaling as the demand grows.”

Study Active

Study Active is the UK’s leading provider of Gym Instructor and Personal Trainer qualifications.

“Julie had a great idea and we were keen to help,” says James Luscombe, Study Active managing director. “Immediately clear to us was the need to work with trusted PTs who would share the vision and be able to produce professional yet personalised sessions: indeed, it’s the personalisation and ‘fitness gift’ to the NHS that set #DoingOurBit apart.”

Active IQ

Active IQ is one of the UK’s leading awarding organisations for the active leisure, learning and wellbeing sector. Its first-rate team of verifiers and assessors have checked every minute of every workout to ensure safety and integrity.

“We set out to help create something personal and friendly but also of the highest quality and integrity,” says Jenny Patrickson, Active IQ managing director. “Our External Verifier team quickly stepped up to share their expertise to check the safety and quality of all the workouts. In this way, we believe #DoingOurBit will stand the test of time and prove valuable to NHS staff long after lockdown.”
