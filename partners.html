Our Partners

Doing Our Bit would not exist without our amazing partners. The generosity of individuals and organisations giving their time and skills has helped us create this unique free platform to support the wellbeing of NHS and social care workers. Added to this, we have a fantastic group of first-rate instructors and experts in fitness, wellness and mental health who have donated their programmes, sessions and advice. We thank them all!

Key Benefits to Partnering with DOB

  1. Support the UK's 1.4m NHS and Social Care staff
  2. Partner with the UK’s largest ‘free to participate’ wellness portal, providing a blueprint for the improved wellbeing of healthcare workers across the UK
  3. Be part of the Preventative Health Care movement, improving the nation's wellbeing now and into the future
  4. Access exclusive NHS Doing Our Bit branded promotional materials to demonstrate your support
  5. Raise awareness of your company's CSR throughout your industry

Sponsorship Packages

We have a variety of sponsorship opportunities available, with additional benefits, but hurry while they last! Please contact us at contact@doingourbit.org.uk to find out more!

All of our sponsorship packages are unique, but can include:

  • Your logo on the Doing Our Bit website Partner carousel and Mind/Body/Fuel/Finances Pages
  • Your logo included on participant emails and the on demand video content hub
  • Social Media shout outs
  • Content collaborations
